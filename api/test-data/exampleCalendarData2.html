Wednesday, 4 JanuaryStory Bridge, Kangaroo Point
Australian Indoor Hockey Championships

The Story Bridge will be lit Teal to support the Australian Indoor Hockey Championships.

The Australian Indoor Hockey Championships is an annual Event comprised of five age groups; U13, U15, U18, U21 & Opens across Male and Female competitions. This Indoor Age Championships is the second biggest championships event hosted by Hockey Australia after the Masters’ event. This event will be at the Brisbane Sports Entertainment Centre Boondall from the 3rd to the 25th January, 2023.

This Light Up is organised by: Brisbane Economic Development Agency

Friday, 20 JanuaryStory Bridge, Kangaroo Point
2023 National Corrections Day

The Story Bridge will be lit Blue and White to support 2023 National Corrections Day.

Queensland Corrective Services (QCS) assists crime prevention through the humane containment, supervision and rehabilitation of prisoners and offenders in correctional centres and the community. National Corrections Day is a day to appreciate and celebrate the work of corrections officers on the front line of public safety the length and breadth of Queensland and across the nation.

This Light Up is organised by: Queensland Corrective Services
